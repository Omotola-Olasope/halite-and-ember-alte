Who We Are

The name says
everything.

Halite
Rock Salt

Enduring, essential, and formed under pressure. The mineral that preserves, connects, and sustains. A symbol of substance and permanence.

Ember
Glowing Fire

Warmth that persists long after the flame. The quiet energy that nurtures, illuminates, and transforms. A symbol of care and possibility.

"We put our customers at the heart of everything we do, creating places and services people are proud to call their own."

Halite & Ember is a multi-disciplinary firm registered in the United Kingdom and Nigeria. We operate at the intersection of technology, property, and people, providing services that enable individuals, businesses, and communities to thrive.

Whether we are deploying IT infrastructure, managing a property portfolio, advising a leadership team, or supporting a growing business, our commitment is the same: rigour, warmth, and integrity in everything we do.

Our Mission

To build ventures and deliver services that create lasting value for clients, partners, and the communities we work in.
